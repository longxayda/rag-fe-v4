## Animation Sequence Details

### Question Transition (0.3s)
1. Current question slides LEFT and fades out
2. New question slides in from RIGHT and fades in
3. Options cascade in one by one (0.1s delay each)
4. Question number badge rotates in

### Answer Selection
1. Click button - scales down (0.98x)
2. Shows checkmark/X with color change
3. Explanation slides down (height animates)
4. Next button fades in from below

### Results Screen (Score â‰¥ 70%)
Timeline:
- 0.0s: Screen fades in
- 0.2s: Circle appears with rotation
- 0.5s: CONFETTI STARTS! ğŸ‰
- 0.7s: Progress circle draws (1.5s duration)
- 0.8s: Trophy spins in
- 0.8s: Score counter starts (0 â†’ final)
- 1.0s: Percentage appears
- 1.5s: Title fades in
- 2.0s: Message appears
- 2.5s: Progress dots pop in
- 3.0s: Buttons fade in
- 3.5s: Confetti ends

### Confetti Details
- Duration: 3 seconds
- Colors: Red and gold (heritage colors)
- Burst from left (10-30%) and right (70-90%)
- Continuous emission
- Particles fall with gravity
- Auto-cleanup after completion

### Social Sharing Flow
1. Click "Share" button
2. Menu slides down from button
3. Click platform option
4. Opens sharing dialog / copies to clipboard
5. Menu slides up and disappears

## Responsive Behavior
- Mobile: Full-width cards, larger tap targets
- Tablet: Medium-width cards, optimized spacing
- Desktop: Maximum width constrained, hover effects enabled

## Dark Mode Support
All animations and colors adapt to dark mode:
- Circle progress: Same animations, different colors
- Confetti: Same colors work in both modes
- Text: Auto-adjusts contrast
- Shadows: Subtle in dark mode
